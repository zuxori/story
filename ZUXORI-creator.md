Sov is a storyteller, poet and creator.

As a writer, he cut his teeth interviewing heavyweight fighters, hedge quants and Nobel laureates. After an extended study, he explored the main conflicts of philosophy, writing and speaking thereafter on human intelligence and meaning at financial and motivational conferences. Artistically, he was one of the first participants in the nascent crypto-art movement, curating the Sovpunk collection of cryptopunks and creating the AI art projects Guardians, Medallions and Quattro.

The themes in his storytelling include love, destiny and the future.

Sov is the creator of ZU X ORI, the epic reincarnation sequel to Shakespeare’s *Romeo and Juliet*.
